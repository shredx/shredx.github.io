ShredX

Always wanted to participate in Open-Source Development with people but never got a chance to start?

SharedX is an great step to get started with Open Source development and contribution. On weekends, group of people come together to execute small tasks they have planned for. It can be your mini project or one of the technology from your BuckList.

ShredX is an initiative by Ashok Dey to collaborate together in open source projects on weekends.
